# Vibe Marketing GTM Playbook (China First)

## 1) Positioning
- Core message:
  - "Upload once, get real CN job links + optimized resume + interview prep."
- Competitive edge:
  - Real-link-first recommendation logic.
  - Mandatory optimized resume output (not hidden behind optional UI).
  - Product + investor readiness telemetry in one stack.

## 2) ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- ICP-1: 统招/海归求职者 (0-3 years) needing structured application speed.
- ICP-2: 转型工程师 (traditional -> AI/data) needing role matching and resume rewrite.
- ICP-3: Small bootcamp/career studios needing B2B tool support.

## 3) Channel Plan
- Short-video:
  - Bilibili, Douyin clips with "before/after resume + real link demo".
- Long-form:
  - Zhihu and WeChat articles with case studies and benchmark comparisons.
- Community:
  - WeChat groups, Xiaohongshu notes, niche job-search communities.
- Enterprise:
  - Direct outreach to bootcamps, universities, small HR teams.

## 4) Content Engine (4-week cadence)
- Week 1: Product trust
  - Demo video: from upload to real-link result.
  - Post: why fake demo jobs kill conversion trust.
- Week 2: Outcome proof
  - User story: optimized resume -> interview callback.
  - Data card: speed, conversion, and quality metrics.
- Week 3: Technical authority
  - Engineering transparency: readiness, tests, uptime.
  - Article: CN hiring data source strategy.
- Week 4: Commercial conversion
  - Offer: trial + guided onboarding.
  - CTA: book pilot / leave enterprise lead.

## 5) Funnel and KPI Targets (30 days)
- Top of funnel:
  - Landing UV >= 5,000
  - Content CTR >= 2.5%
- Activation:
  - Upload-to-process completion >= 55%
  - First-session "download result" >= 30%
- Revenue prep:
  - Qualified leads >= 50
  - Pilot meetings >= 12
- Trust:
  - Recommendation real-link ratio >= 95%
  - Critical incident count = 0

## 6) Experiment Backlog
- Experiment A: Hero headline variants (outcome-driven vs speed-driven)
- Experiment B: CTA location (top only vs top+mid)
- Experiment C: Social proof block (with vs without metrics proof)
- Experiment D: pricing teaser (trial framing vs ROI framing)

## 7) Weekly Review Template
- What moved:
  - Growth metrics delta, wins, losses.
- What broke:
  - Channel issues, product friction, data reliability.
- Next week:
  - 3 priority experiments + owners + expected impact.
